Our Heritage

The Town That Weaves Gold

Mangalagiri, nestled by the sacred hills of Andhra Pradesh, is famed for its distinctive Nizam-style borders and feather-light cotton. Every saree is woven on a traditional pit loom, dyed by hand, and finished with shimmering zari.

When you wear THE MANGALAGIRI WEAVES, you carry forward a craft that has clothed generations in quiet luxury.
